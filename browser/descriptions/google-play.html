Free Online Office: LibreOffice, OpenOffice & more

LibreOffice based editor for Microsoft Office, LibreOffice and OpenOffice docs.

Free Online Office is a text editor, spreadsheet and presentation program based on LibreOffice, the world's most popular Open Source office suite - and now it's on Android, enhancing your possibilities to work on mobile and for collaboration.

This app is in active development, feedback and bug reports are very welcome.

Supported files:

  • Open Document Format (.odt, .odp, .ods, .ots, .ott, .otp)
  • Microsoft Office 2007/2010/2013/2016/2019 (.docx, .pptx, .xlsx, .dotx, .xltx, .ppsx)
  • Microsoft Office 97/2000/XP/2003 (.doc, .ppt, .xls, .dot, .xlt, .pps)

Report issues:

Use the bugtracker and attach any files that caused problems via https://github.com/freeonlineoffice/online/issues. Please note that anything you enter in the bugtracker will be public.

About the app:

Free Online Office for Android uses the same engine as LibreOffice for Windows, Mac, and Linux. This, combined with a new front-end based on Free Online Office, reads and saves documents similarly to LibreOffice desktop.
